About Me

Hello! I'm Aditi Chandra, a Robotics & Automation undergraduate at The Neotia University with a strong passion for building intelligent systems that combine robotics, embedded systems, artificial intelligence, and automation.


My journey in robotics started with curiosity about how machines interact with the real world and has grown into hands-on experience developing autonomous and IoT-enabled solutions. I enjoy working on projects that solve real-world challenges in agriculture, surveillance, and smart systems through the integration of hardware and software.


Over the years, I have worked on projects involving computer vision, machine learning, embedded systems, IoT, and autonomous robotics. My most notable achievement is leading the development of RAKSHAK, a multi-terrain amphibious surveillance rover that won the IEEE Region 10 Robotics Competition 2025.


Beyond academics, I am a National-Level Taekwondo Champion, an artist, and a photography enthusiast. These experiences have helped me develop discipline, creativity, teamwork, and problem-solving skills that I apply to both engineering projects and leadership roles.


I am currently focused on advancing my knowledge in robotics, AI-powered systems, computer vision, and autonomous technologies while seeking opportunities to contribute to innovative projects and research in the field of intelligent automation.
